Course Overview

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Ministry Studies program at Kingdom Soldiers Ministerial College offers a comprehensive and interdisciplinary approach to preparing students for various leadership roles within Christian ministry. This program is designed to provide a solid foundation in theological education, practical ministry skills, and spiritual formation, equipping graduates for effective service in church, para-church, and community contexts.

The curriculum of the BA in Ministry Studies typically includes a diverse range of courses covering biblical studies, theology, church history, pastoral care, counseling, preaching, evangelism, missions, leadership, and discipleship. These courses aim to provide students with a broad understanding of Christian faith, doctrine, and practice, while also allowing them to explore specific areas of interest within ministry. In addition to academic coursework, students in this program may participate in practical ministry experiences, internships, and fieldwork to apply their learning in real-world settings. These hands-on opportunities allow students to develop their ministry skills, leadership abilities, and interpersonal communication in diverse ministry contexts under the guidance of experienced mentors and instructors.

Throughout the program, emphasis is placed on spiritual formation, character development, and personal growth. Students are encouraged to cultivate a vibrant spiritual life through prayer, worship, and engagement with scripture, fostering a deepening relationship with God and a commitment to servant leadership. Upon completion of the BA in Ministry Studies, graduates are prepared for a wide range of ministry vocations and leadership roles within the Christian community. This could include positions as pastors, youth pastors, worship leaders, church planters, missionaries, chaplains, counselors, or leaders in nonprofit organizations.

Overall, the BA in Ministry Studies at Kingdom Soldiers Ministerial College aims to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and spiritual maturity needed to serve effectively in ministry, advancing the Kingdom of God and impacting lives for Christ in a variety of contexts
