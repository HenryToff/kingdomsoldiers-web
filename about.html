About Us

Discover Our Background

Enoba JB Event Center is a versatile facility located in Afari, near Abuakwa in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. The center offers a range of amenities, including a banquet hall suitable for various events, a local and continental restaurant, a bar, a poolside area with an additional bar, a private pool, a guest house, and a gym. offering a comprehensive range of facilities to cater to diverse needs. Our center is designed to provide guests with exceptional experiences, whether they're attending an event, dining, or seeking relaxation.
